Grass

Personalize your growing experience

Are you tired of your plants dying despite everything you do to take care of it? Use Grass!

Grass keeps you on top of your watering and fertilization needs, keeping your plant happy and alive.

Features:

Sends timely notifications for you to water your plants in a timely manner.

Colourful dashboards with straight-to-the-point visuals so you can spend less time jumping around menus to find what you need.

You get to increase the greenery in your home / working place!

This app was created by Saumil Anand, James Kuang Zhongchuan, Jeong Wonho & Loh Hao Yuan under the 2022 Swift Accelerator Programme.
